Services

Not Your Grandfather's Barbershop
The Crew Cuts emphasizes a customer-centric service where the customers needs are always the priority.

Man’s Haircuts

A standard cut for a shorter, traditional men's style employing the use of shears or clippers as the stylist sees fit, then cleaning the neck line with clippers.

Beard Trim

Whether you have a soul patch, goatee or full beard, our barbers will give it that sharp edge and style that can only be achieved by a straight razor in the hand of a professional.

Mustache Trim

Using clippers or shears to trim or shape mustache. Finished with a relaxing hot towel. (If requested in advance and at an additional cost, a razor line up of the jaw, neck and cheeks may be completed by one of our Master Barbers).

Shave

Using pre-shave oils and hot towels to prepare the skin for an old school straight razor shave, finished with a facial massage, after shave moisturizer and even more relaxing hot towels.
